Santorini

Santorini is a natural wonder of the world, for its world famous volcano which formed a beautiful island. It has become a major site of attraction to alot of tourists around world inculding big names and celebrities. Its all white surrounding brings lots of peace and calmness.

Go

Santorini as an island was from massive volcanic eruption, many thousands of years ago. Many tourists from all over the world come to Santorini for holidays and relaxation, and its current shape and geomorphology was from the eruption. The Santorini sunset is something behold the natural orange view resting on the sea is some beautiful experience. There are many sunset cruises to choose from and the sailing itinerary is packed with fun activities like hiking around the volcano, swimming in the hot springs, and ending the day with the legendary sunset, depending on your preference, you can book a private catamaran or even a multi-day sailing trip and watch multiple sunsets for that once-in-a-lifetime special occasion. Imerovigli lies between its two bustling neighbors Oia and Fira. This clifftop village is an underrated lookout point as its more secluded, thus offering an intimate sunset experience. You can find many hidden corners to take photos of the Santorini sunset without heads blocking your shots. Panagia The oskepasti Chapel is popular with visitors, especially couples on the quest for the perfect Santorini wedding photoshoot.
